# Bug Reports

## Bug 1
Bug ID: BUG-001  
Title: Submit button not clickable on mobile

Environment:
- Browser: Chrome
- Device: Android

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Open the website on a mobile device
2. Navigate to the Contact page
3. Tap the Submit button

Expected Result:
The form should be submitted successfully.

Actual Result:
The Submit button does not respond.

Severity: High  
Priority: High

## Bug 2
Bug ID: BUG-002  
Title: Password field allows less than required characters

Environment:
- Browser: Chrome (Desktop)

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Open the Registration page
2. Enter fewer than 6 characters in the password field
3. Submit the form

Expected Result:
A validation message should be displayed.

Actual Result:
The form is submitted without validation.

Severity: Medium  
Priority: Medium

---
